About the App

ဓမ္မ အရသာ (Dhamma A Yatha) is designed to support meditation practice and Buddhist Dhamma learning for everyday users. The app focuses on mindful living, inner calm, and accessible guidance that helps practitioners stay grounded and consistent.

Features

  • Simple and focused meditation learning experience
  • Buddhist Dhamma content for mindfulness and reflection
  • Lightweight Android-first design for daily use
  • Clean interface built for clarity and calm
